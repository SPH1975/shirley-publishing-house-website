About us

Publishing with purpose and personal guidance.

Shirley Publishing House supports authors, researchers, educators, schools, and institutions through practical, professional publishing services.

Who we are

Helping ideas become credible, polished publications.

Based in Kasibu, Nueva Vizcaya, Shirley Publishing House provides editorial, academic, registration, design, printing, binding, and repository services for clients across the Philippines.

We recognize that every manuscript has a different purpose. Some authors need a complete publishing pathway, while others need only editing, registration, design, or printing. Our process begins with understanding the work and recommending only the services it requires.

  • Clear assessment before work begins
  • Project-specific quotation and timeline
  • Author approval at key stages
  • Professional presentation and production

Our commitment

Quality publications that leave lasting impressions.

We combine careful handling, practical guidance, and professional production so authors can present their work with confidence.

What guides us

Mission, vision, and core values

M

Mission

To make professional publishing more accessible through reliable editorial, registration, production, and author-support services.

V

Vision

To be a trusted Philippine publishing partner known for quality, integrity, responsiveness, and practical support for diverse works.

I

Integrity

We communicate clearly, respect authorship, protect manuscript confidentiality, and handle every project responsibly.

Q

Quality

We prioritize careful review, clean design, readable formatting, and professional production standards.

S

Service

We provide direct guidance and respond to the specific needs, objectives, and budget of each publication.
