Frequently asked

Before requesting a quotation

Can I request only one service?

Yes. You may request editing, registration, layout, printing, binding, or another individual service.

Can you assess an unfinished manuscript?

Yes, but the quotation may change if the final length or required work differs significantly.

Do you accept thesis-derived articles?

Yes. The work should be rewritten as a standalone scholarly article rather than submitted as the full thesis.

Can my publication appear in the repository?

Eligible works published through Shirley Publishing House may be added with title, author, cover, description, and access link.

How are prices determined?

Pricing depends on manuscript condition, page count, design, registration, printing specifications, quantity, and schedule.

How do I send my file?

Use the submission form to prepare an email, then attach the manuscript before sending it.
